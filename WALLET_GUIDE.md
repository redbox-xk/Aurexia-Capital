## Managing Your Wallets

### What is a Wallet?

A wallet is your secure container for digital assets. Think of it as a bank account, but one that you control completely. Flash Wallet lets you create unlimited wallets for different purposes.

**Use cases:**
- **Savings Wallet** - Long-term holdings
- **Trading Wallet** - Active trading and movement
- **Savings Wallet** - Emergency fund
- **Investment Wallet** - Specific investments

### Creating a New Wallet

1. Go to **Wallets** section
2. Click **Create New Wallet**
3. Enter a memorable name (e.g., "My Savings")
4. Choose wallet type (optional metadata)
5. Click **Create**
6. Your wallet address is instantly generated

### Setting Your Primary Wallet

Your primary wallet is where funds are sent by default when you receive payments. To set a primary:

1. Open **Wallets**
2. Find the wallet you want as primary
3. Click **Set as Primary**
4. Confirm your choice

**Tip**: Change your primary wallet anytime based on your current needs.

### Wallet Address

Each wallet has a unique address. This is your digital identity for that wallet. Share this address to receive funds from anyone in the world.

**Security**: Your address is public and safe to share. Your private keys never leave your computer.

## Sending Funds

### Step-by-Step: How to Send

1. **Go to Send**
   - Click "Send" in main navigation
   - Or click "Send" on any wallet

2. **Select Source Wallet**
   - Choose which wallet to send from
   - Verify your balance

3. **Enter Recipient Address**
   - Paste or type the recipient's wallet address
   - Double-check the address carefully
   - Copy from your saved contacts for accuracy

4. **Enter Amount**
   - Type the amount you wish to send
   - System shows fee estimate
   - Verify total amount

5. **Review Transaction**
   - Confirm all details one final time
   - Check recipient address matches
   - Verify amount is correct

6. **Send**
   - Click "Send Funds"
   - Transaction processes instantly
   - You'll see confirmation immediately

### Smart Features

**Contact Saving**: Save frequently-used recipient addresses for quick access later.

**Fee Estimation**: See exactly what your transaction will cost before committing.

**Amount Suggestions**: Quick buttons for common amounts (25%, 50%, 75%, Max).

## Common Questions

### What if I forget my password?

1. Click "Sign In"
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email
4. Click link in confirmation email
5. Set new password

### Can I recover a sent transaction?

Transactions are final once confirmed. Always verify the recipient address before sending.

### How do I delete a wallet?

1. Go to Wallets
2. Click wallet options (three dots)
3. Select Delete
4. Confirm deletion

**Warning**: Make sure you've moved any funds elsewhere first.

### Why is there a fee on my transaction?

Transaction fees go to network validators who process your payment. Fees vary based on network congestion.

### How long does a transaction take?

Most transactions complete within seconds to minutes, depending on network conditions. You'll see real-time status updates.

### Can I change my primary wallet?

Yes, anytime! Go to Wallets, find your desired primary wallet, and click "Set as Primary."

### Is my data private?

Yes. Your data is encrypted and protected by Row Level Security. Only you can access your account and wallets.

### What if I have a problem?

Contact our support team via the help section in your profile. We're here to help.
